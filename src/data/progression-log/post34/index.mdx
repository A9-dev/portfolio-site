### formatjsonl.com

Finished `formatjsonl.com`, a small JSON Lines formatter.

It lets you paste raw JSONL, upload a `.jsonl`, `.ndjson`, or `.json` file, drag files into the editor, and copy the formatted output back out.

Project post: [formatjsonl.com writeup](/projects/formatjsonl)

Repository: [A9-dev/formatjsonl](https://github.com/A9-dev/formatjsonl)

Deployment: [formatjsonl.com](https://formatjsonl.com/)
